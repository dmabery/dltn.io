From here: https://www.reddit.com/r/ObsidianMD/comments/16kfbk5/my_vault_template/

[[Stephan Ango]] on how he uses his Obsidian vault

>Load up the brain via podcasts, videos, travel, books, conversations. Defrag the brain in nature or in Obsidian.
>
>Inspiration is the feeling of going from loading to defragging.

This makes a ton of sense, and why *creating* need to be a part of one's note-taking apparatus just as much as *organizing* is.

Gather information from content, organize into evergreen ideas, create and think.
